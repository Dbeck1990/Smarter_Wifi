Do you supply me with a 'wireless' Router?

Purple WIFI is a software provider, so we do not supply hardware. If you would like to be redirected to your nearest Purple WIFI reseller to discuss hardware/equipment options then please raise a ticker advising of your location.

If you have an existing broadband connection, it may be possible you already have a compastible router in place which could support or act as an overlay for Purple WIFI. please reveiw our list of compatible devices.
